Personas

Ms. Jessica Bard

Jessica runs a small breakfast and lunch café. She is looking for connects with local businesses and farms that could work with her to supply her café. She is would also like to be more involved in business legislation and is hoping to find others who are concerned as well.

Dr. George Owens

George recently took over a TMJ practice and wants to create relationships with other doctors and dentist in the area to work with and refer patients to. Being new to the area as well he is interested in local events and wants to be an asset to the community.

Scenarios

  • New medical practice/doctor looking to connect with other medical professionals in the area.
  • Small business owner finding information for meetings with other owners on business issues within the community.
  • Searching for businesses willing to sponsor community youth baseball teams.
  • Local florist in looking for photographers to work with on weddings.
  • Are there any upcoming events being being held at local businesses?
