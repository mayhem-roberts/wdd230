Non-Profit

- Business name and description on our website directory.
- Post your events on the Chamber’s online Calendar of Events.
- Monthly e-newsletter with Chamber events and community news.
- Events where you can interact with other members.

Bronze

- Listing on website directory including logo and website link.
- Eligible to host events and sponsor Chamber events.
- Eligible to place ads in the e-newsletter.
- Meet with Chamber staff to strategize opportunities and resources.

Silver

- Free booth at town events.
- Tickets to monthly Chamber luncheons.
- Referrals from Chamber staff and volunteers.
- Events where you can network with members of other chambers.

Gold

- First for sponsorships at Chamber’s signature events.
- Annual meeting with Executive Director and Board Officer.
- Advertisement on Chamber’s home page website.
- Featured monthly in Chamber’s news letter.
